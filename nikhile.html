nikhilE is a tool to transliterate Telugu text into English. It is not a translator. In other words, it just gives English spellings of Telugu text. It is useful to you if you can understand Telugu but cannot read Telugu. Or, you can use it when you want to read Telugu text and your computer is not configured to display Telugu. So you will always be able to read any Telugu message sent to you. And, it’s free!

If you want to convert from English to Telugu, go to Lekhini.
